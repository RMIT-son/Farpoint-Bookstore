Privacy Policy

Farpoint Bookstore is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy tells you about our online collection and use of data. The terms of this policy apply to all Farpoint Bookstore websites, unless different terms are specified in a form or contract provided to you online or offline.

By using this site, you understand and agree to the terms of this policy. This site is owned by Farpoint Bookstore and may be accessed in Vietnam and abroad. For data protection purposes, Farpoint Bookstore is the controller and, unless otherwise noted, is also the processor of data. Information collected may be retained, and may be stored, processed, accessed, and used in jurisdictions whose privacy laws may be different and less protective than those of your home jurisdiction.

Farpoint Bookstore collects information you provide, such as when you create an account, make a purchase, sign up to receive emails, or interact with Farpoint Bookstore through social media. The types of personal information collected may include:

  • Information you provide when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Information you provide when you make a purchase, such as your shipping address and payment information.
  • Information you provide when you sign up to receive emails, such as your email address.
  • Information you provide when you interact with Farpoint Bookstore through social media, such as your social media handle or username.

Farpoint Bookstore also automatically collects information about how you access and use our websites, such as your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamps, and related metadata. We use this information alone or in combination with other information to improve our websites and to prevent fraud. Some of this information may be collected using cookies and similar tracking technology, as explained further under the heading “Cookies and Similar Tracking Technology.”

Farpoint Bookstore uses the information we collect to provide you products, offers, and services, to deliver to you advertisements, offers or other content tailored to your interests, and to conduct other Farpoint Bookstore business operations. Farpoint Bookstore may use the information to communicate with you about, and administer your participation in, special events, contests, sweepstakes, programs, surveys and other offers. Farpoint Bookstore may also use the information to respond to your customer service inquiries or requests, to customize your experience on the Farpoint Bookstore websites, to serve you personalized ads, or to inform you of updates to our privacy policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@farpointbookstore.com.
